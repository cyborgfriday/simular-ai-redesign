# Simular Design Challenge: Product Design Audit

## What to Do
Spend ~2 hours doing a design audit of Simular Cloud (public beta).

**At minimum:**
- Your take on who this product is for
- The most important UX or product issues you notice
- Ideas for how you'd improve things

**You can zoom in on:**
- A core user flow
- A specific use case or user type
- A moment that felt confusing, delightful, or broken

No need to cover everything — depth > breadth.

## Deliverable
Flexible format. Must be clear on:
- What you focused on (and why)
- What you noticed
- What you'd change
- Any assumptions made along the way

## What They're Looking For
- Product thinking and design judgment
- How you prioritize and make tradeoffs
- Comfort with ambiguity
- Clear communication

**Not judging:** pixel-perfect visuals, time spent past 2 hours, whether you "got it right"

## Time Box
~2 hours. Don't over-invest.
